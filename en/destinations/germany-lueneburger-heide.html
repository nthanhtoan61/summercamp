Our Camp Adventure Academy is located almost halfway between Bremen, Hamburg and Hanover in the beautiful Lüneburger Heide. The site is our biggest international summer camp in Northern Germany, Niedersachsen

Within walking distance of an old traditional North German village with farms and half-timbered houses, we have space on more than 150,000 m². The Academy is divided into the activity worlds Beach & Water, Survival and Creative. Each area offers space for countless exciting activities. Whether kayaking, jumping on the water trampoline, building rafts, survival training with shelter building and fire making or climbing in the high ropes course; adventure seekers and creative types will get their money's worth here.

Accommodation & meals in the tent camp or the Adventure Lodges

At our international vacation camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between accommodation in tents and cabins when you register. Our seniors sleep in their own tent village separately from the juniors, so that both age groups have their own tent village. From the two tent villages and the huts it's not far to the wash houses and toilets. The sanitary facilities are separate for boys and girls. The supervisors, our “teamers”, live right next door and are there for you around the clock if you have any questions, requests or problems - 24 hours a day!

Tent Village - for that very special adventure feeling

Our tent villages have tents with six to seven sleeping places. Equipped with a wooden floor and gallery, they invite you to spend the night in comfort. Children can make themselves wonderfully comfortable here with a sleeping bag and sleeping mat.

Adventure Lodges - just like in Canada

Our cabins are each equipped with four to eight beds - of course the shared rooms are separated by gender. There are shelves and seating available in the rooms. The overnight stay in the hut is charged extra at USD 120 (per week).

Catering

We cook fresh - always! We don't serve packet soup and boring food. Our menus are freshly prepared. Of course, we serve fresh bread rolls, cold cuts and a wide selection of fruit and cereals at breakfast. We have juices and tea to drink. During the day, water with or without sparkling water is available from our water machines and, of course, fresh fruit is always available for the first hunger pangs. At lunchtime and in the evening, we usually cook hot meals - with the exception of snacks. Of course, water and fresh salads from the buffet are also available for these meals.

Summer camp program and excursions

If you have the choice, you're spoiled for choice - on arrival, the children choose the activities they are most interested in from a wide range of options. This means that they design their leisure program at camp according to their own wishes and ideas. Examples of projects are Sports and Action, Adventure, Creative, Survival, to name but a few. In addition to the courses, there will be other activities that we will experience together with all the campers. The group games are particularly fun and you will wish there was no end to them. The entire camp facility is available for this. The football pitch also offers plenty of space for fun and action. In the evenings, we like to sit around the campfire and play the guitar or eat marshmallows.

A special highlight for those who stay at the camp for two weeks is a joint excursion on Saturday. They will either go to the Serengeti Park (Juniors) / Heide Park (Seniors) or to the “Verwell” adventure pool - real highlights in northern Germany. The campers can choose for themselves on site. Those staying in camp for two weeks can also take part in a two-day adventure hike at the weekend. Alternatively, we offer a day trip to Hamburg, which costs an extra USD65 and must be booked in advance.

Serengeti Park

Our trip to the Serengeti Park in Hodenhagen will be an exciting experience. For a whole day, the children can admire lions, monkeys and rare animal species in outdoor enclosures. The park also offers great carousels, shows and a jeep safari, for example. The black and white striped zebra double-decker bus allows you to explore previously unknown animal worlds.

Heide Park

Our trip to Heide Park in Soltau will be an exciting adventure. Whether it's the Dive Coaster, bobsleigh run, mountain rafting or suspended looping track, there's a whole day to try everything out. The park also offers great shows and some interesting activities. As adventurers, children will experience unforgettable moments here that they will remember for a long time to come.

adventure pool Verwell

The trip to the “Verwell” adventure pool in Verden is in no way inferior to this. A great leisure pool with a giant slide, an indoor adventure pool with a current channel and lots of other attractions - let's go for a day of swimming fun!

Adventure Hike

There is also a special highlight for all participants who have booked one of our two-week camps - the Adventure Hike with a weekend overnight stay in the great outdoors. An extra dose of adventure!

Citytrip Hamburg

Instead of doing the hike, there is also the option of taking part in our city trip to Hamburg. This option has been specially designed for international campers, with lots of sightseeing, but it might also be interesting for the German kids who live a little further away. Of course there is also time for a shopping spree! The trip costs USD65 extra.

Summer camp courses

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Adventure, Sport & Creative

If you want to experience exciting things and enjoy creative, sporting and/or adventurous activities, then the Adventure, Sports and Creative Camp is the right place for you. Everyone chooses the program they like according to their personal taste and interests: sometimes spontaneously, when it comes to interesting workshops, fun games in between or a few hours at the lake, but sometimes over several days, when we dive into our project worlds and start joint activities.


7-12 years & 12-16 years

English/German

In addition to interesting language lessons in the Camp Adventure style, we focus on discovering new cultures with children and young people from all over the world. We teach at least 12 x 45 min per week in small groups. There is also the opportunity to use and improve foreign language skills outside of the language profiles, as the children are looked after bilingually by our team members.

12-16 years

Lifeguarding

Heroes of the water wanted! You see them almost everywhere where there is water. At the edge of the pool, on high seats on the beach or even on boats at the edge of the non-swimmers' area - the lifeguards. Mostly unnoticed, they ensure safety and in emergency situations they intervene and even save lives - real everyday heroes! We offer the opportunity to take the official lifeguard course during your stay with us.

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Survival

Over the course of the week, the children learn basic skills that have often ensured the survival of many others. For example, we coach them in the correct use of knives. Things also get hot: nothing is more important in the wildernessthan a fire. It serves as protection from wild animals, provides warmth and is of course used to prepare food. How do you make a nice campfire without matches or a lighter? Our coaches will demonstrate this and how to cook in nature. The children are also prepared for an adventurous water crossing and the construction of a protective shelter is also part of the survival skills.

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Soccer

Whether technique, endurance, strength or tactics training, your children will also be trained in fair play and team spirit. But don't worry, playing soccer is of course not neglected. Fun and enjoyment are at the forefront of age-appropriate training, which is why the ball always plays a central role in all exercises. Our soccer profile is aimed at all football-loving kids, newcomers and club players who want a bit of adventure as well as soccer!

7-12 years

Horseback riding

Our riding profile is just right for animal lovers! Together with our partner, the Reitsportverein Altenwahlingen e.V., we offer your children varied riding lessons on 6 excellently trained horses. You will learn both how to handle the animals safely and how to work with them as partners - both on and off the horse. Of course, we will also take care of the horsesand prepare them for training. At the end of the week we will ride in one of the most beautiful landscapes in Germany - the south of the Lüneburg Heath. The riding lessons are led by qualified and certified trainers with many years of experience in teaching children and young people to ride. We can therefore guarantee optimum support and training. This profile is not suitable for advanced riders. However, if you are a beginner with some experience, the week is a good opportunity to improve your riding skills.

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Husky Camp

Spring/Autumn
Driving a sled dog team - isn't that what many people dream of? In our spring/autumn husky camp you have the opportunity. Not only will you learn the most important things about our great huskies, but also everything about the sleds on wheels, the necessary equipment, the training of the dogs and the special feeding, but you will also have the opportunity to try everything out for yourself and get your so-called “musher's license” at the end. “Musher” - that's what a sled dog handler is called. Adventure, action and enjoyment are on the program. Alexandra and the sled dog team “Lumirunners” are looking forward to the adventure with you!

Summer
Our husky camp is just the thing for dog lovers or those who want to become one! From the peculiarities of the original dogs, the differences to “normal house dogs”, general important facts about dogs to the small 1x1 of sled dogs, you will learn everything you need to get your “husky license” at the end of your husky camp. Of course, cuddling, feeding, learning the language of dogs and joint excursions are also very important! The program includes trekking tours, splashing around in the water, playing and making real friends with your husky! Alexandra and the sled dog team “Lumirunners” are looking forward to seeing you!

12-16 years

Fishing

If you want to experience nature from one of its quieter sides, our fishing camp is the right place for you. In this course, we will prepare you for the fishing exam together. You will learn about fishing and fishing techniques in general and can apply what you have learned while fishing together at our fishing pond. After the big catch, our trained supervisors will also go through the basics of gutting with you and show you a few delicious ways to prepare fish. Of course, the topic of animal and species protection will also be covered. Even if you are an advanced angler, you are welcome to join us for a few hours of fishing. So then, good luck fishing!

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Dancing

For the dance enthusiasts among you, we finally have the right course for you. In our additional dance course, we will show you different street dance styles and work with you to create your own choreography to match your chosen hip-hop song. In 12 45-minute lessons, we will try out new steps with you, rehearse a short performance and show you the fun that rhythmic movement can be.

7-12 years & 12-16 years

Creative Camp

If you enjoy crafting and designing, then you are sure to have fun at our creative camp. Our international teamers will make and decorate lots of beautiful things with you. Together you will design jewelry and create new things from old ones in a process known as upcycling. We will paint with watercolors on canvases and create a joint work of art using a wide variety of natural materials such as wood and stone. If you want to try out something new creatively, this is the right course for you.

15-17 years

Senior Plus/Leadership

You think the camp is cool, but would prefer to spend your time with young people of the same age from all over the world? Then the Senior Plus program is just right for you and you even have some privileges: exclusive night action such as flashlight canoeing or moonlight climbing, a later night's sleep and you stay in tents specially reserved for your age group or in our Academy with its own chill-out area.

Choose your favorite profile, e.g. Creative & Adventure Camp with many daily changing outdoor activities and creative leisure activities or a language camp if you want to polish up your German or English language skills with native speakers. You then take part in the profiles in the mornings together with the senior campers.

Or you can opt for the Leadership Camp, which also takes place in the mornings, but is offered exclusively for the Senior Plus age group of 15-17 years. In terms of content, the aim is to develop leadership qualities entirely in English. Whether presentation courses or workshops, e.g. on reflection and team leading - we will introduce you to lots of exciting content that is guaranteed to help you in your personal development! Important: a crucial part of our Leadership Profile is the 3-day overnight hike that takes you to the North Sea and brings with it a number of challenges. The perfect challenge for those of you who are looking for a new “once in a lifetime” challenge!

Of course, our philosophy of “No drugs, no alcohol - just fun!” and our “DigitalDetox” approach of only handing out cell phones and electronic devices during the siesta also apply here. So you have the rest of the day all to yourselves and your “peers” and make friends that may last a lifetime!

17-19 years

International Camp Counselor in Training (ICIT)

If your child has already been to camp as a participant and now wants to become a camp counselor themselves, then our ICIT program is just right for them. This program is aimed at young people between the ages of 17 and 19 who would like to work with children in an international environment and who have at least a basic knowledge of English. After successfully completing the two-week training course, ICITs receive a certificate proving that they have been trained as an International Camp Counselor Assistant (junior staff member). With this qualification, they can already be employed as a counselor assistant (17 years) or as a counselor (18 years and older) in our camps. The training also includes training as a first aider.

Would you like to make new friends?

Come along to our international camp with children and young people from 64 different nations who are spending the summer of their lives together.

Typical day at camp

What does a typical day in Walsrode look like?
How is the daily schedule organized, what times apply – and what can you expect in addition to your chosen program focus?
You can find an overview of excursions, weekend trips, and many other highlights here:

*There will be just as many “non-classical” theme days, sleep-in days and special days at the camp. We always adapt to the weather and the mood! In addition, there can always be spontaneous adjustments to the daily schedule for various reasons, so this is only a guide.

Morning Sports/Polarbear Action

This is of course optional. From looking for frogs to jogging, yoga or fitness, there is a great program for all early risers to choose from every morning.

Breakfast

Our breakfast always consists of a selection of rolls and bread as well as various cold cuts and spreads (jams, honey, chocolate spread etc.) and a wide range of cereals and fruit. Of course, all drinks are always included.

Clean Up

Here we keep our campsite clean together. Whether it's community service (clearing plates, cleaning tables, washing dishes), keeping the sanitary facilities clean or tidying up our own tents - we work together to ensure that everyone feels comfortable.

Mittagessen

Lunch always consists of a hot meal and a salad bar and we always serve water with the meal. At mealtimes, the supervisors and children sit together at one table. We always offer vegetarian and pork-free food.

Siesta

Here, campers can chill out together in the room, lounge or on the cozy wooden benches in the outdoor area. Of course, they can also take an afternoon nap, read, write letters or chat. This gives the children time together to relax a little and recharge their batteries for the rest of the day.

Freesports

Freesports means that all camp participants meet up and everyone can take part in different activities every day. These can be: Soccer, volleyball, archery, letter writing, hiking or simply chilling out in the grass.

Dinner

For dinner, our camp chef will cook for us again (we will always offer vegetarian and pork-free food).

Evening program

The evening program varies greatly and depends on the weather and fitness of the group. Camp group games together with all children, our 'Special Nights' such as wellness night, casino evening or movie night and other programs are possible. The evening program always takes place together with all children - including the 2-week participants. We therefore strive to offer a varied program.

Good night

We always want the participants to get enough rest and sleep. That's why our counselors put the children to bed with a very special Camp Adventure routine. This gives the children the opportunity to reflect on the day and then dream about the day while reading a book or listening to music.

Airport Transfer

Here you can choose between our shared transfer for USD85 each way and a private transfer for USD350 each way.

Collective transfer

With this shuttle, all children are first picked up at the exit directly behind the baggage carousel and then taken to the camp together. This can lead to waiting times.

Our team will be at the airport from 10 a.m. to collect all arriving children. For parents who accompany their children to Hamburg Airport themselves, we offer a central meeting point (at the Rakete) at the McDonald's restaurant at Hamburg Airport. Depending on the number of participants, the transfer can take place by bus, train or car. The shuttle will leave the airport with all children and supervisors in the direction of the camp by 4 p.m. at the latest.

All flights landing later than 3 p.m. cannot be considered for this transfer. In this case we recommend that you book our private transfer.
If your flight lands before 10 a.m., we will have to send an extra supervisor to the airport earlier to pick up your child at the exit after the baggage carousel. This also applies to the return journey if the flight departs later than 6 pm. We charge an extra USD50 for this service, in addition to the normal transfer costs. These costs cover the service of picking up your child earlier/checking them in later and looking after them.

Private transfer

This transfer is recommended if the landing time on the day of arrival is later than 3 p.m., the departure time on the day of departure is before 2 p.m., the arrival or departure is to take place on other days or you do not want your children to have to wait at the airport.
