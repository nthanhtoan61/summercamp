Welcome to our new Beach Camp in Barcelona for Teens (10-16 years)

Ahoy, young sailors and sea enthusiasts! Get ready for an unforgettable nautical journey at our Beach Camp in the heart of Barcelona, Spain. Embark on an exciting adventure where the sea becomes your playground, and the wind guides your sails.

Let the waves of the ocean be your companions as you explore the wonders of Barcelona with new friends. But that's not all! Our camp not only offers thrilling sailing and windsurfing adventures but also the opportunity to improve your language skills in English or Spanish. Immerse yourself in a language course to expand your foreign language knowledge and discover new cultural horizons. 

For participants passionate about football, in addition to water sports activities and language courses, there is also the option to join the Soccer course hosted by the C.E.Vila Olímpica and their professional team of coaches! There, you can showcase your soccer skills. Put on your football boots and demonstrate your abilities on the field to become a true football champion.

Get ready for an unforgettable experience at our Beach Camp in Barcelona! Every minute here will turn into an adventure that will stay with you. We look forward to spending an unforgettable time by the ocean with you!

CAMP LOCATION

Accommodation at BISC - Your home directly at the ocean!

Our base camp is the renowned Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC), nestled along the captivating coast of Barcelona. BISC provides a secure and vibrant environment for our young sailors. Check out their accommodations here to see the comfort and safety that awaits you during your stay.

All rooms have seaview and sleep 4-12 campers on modern bunk beds.

Imagine waking up to the gentle sound of waves and the refreshing sea breeze. BISC offers top-notch facilities, ensuring that your time off the water is just as enjoyable as your time on it. Comfortable rooms, delicious meals, and a lively atmosphere await, making it the perfect setting for forging lifelong friendships.

Please note: The organizer of this trip is our Spanish company Outdoor Education Spain S.L.

Program

Camp Life – Where Adventure Meets Fun

Wondering what a day in our camp looks like? Dive into an exciting world where you can explore new activities every day, all designed to have loads of fun and create unforgettable memories.

Our Barcelona Beach Camp offers a variety of different activities, including sports and action like beach volleyball or a round of Capture the Flag, creative workshops, and ocean swimming, to name a few upcoming activities we offer. These activities allow you to fill your day with fun and adventures, even if you choose not to enroll in an additional course. Boredom is not on our agenda!

Safety is a top priority at the Beach Camp. Our experienced team and certified water sports instructors are available 24/7 to ensure a worry-free experience for participants and parents. From morning sports to evening campfires, every moment is geared towards personal growth, teamwork, and of course, loads of fun.

Why You Should Choose the Beach Camp?

  • Expert Instruction: Learn from certified water sports instructors with years of experience, guiding you from a beginner to an experienced sailor and surfer.
  • Cultural Immersion: Discover the rich culture of Barcelona and learn more about its history, cuisine, and vibrant traditions.
  • Friendships: Form new friendships with fellow adventurers from around the world and share the excitement of the open sea.
  • Activities: Experience our diverse activities on, in, and beside the water. We have the right one for you too.
  • Safety Comes First: Our dedicated team takes care of the well-being of every participant on and off the water.

Ready for the adventure of a lifetime? Secure your spot today and join us for an unforgettable experience in the heart of Barcelona. 

Spots are limited, so don't miss your opportunity - reserve now!

Course Overview 

In the mornings:

Beach, Adventure, Skateboarding and Creative

This profile is for all those not interested in the language, scuba diving or football course. Every day we will offer new activities on the beach and inside the BISC. If you bring your own skateboard/inline equipment really nice urban skate spots will wait for you to be explored!

Course details: 

  • Duration: 5 days, 3h a day
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Language Courses

Spanish: we will offer a Spanish language class for beginners/intermediates. Like always in a playful way just to tune in and get to know the language better. The best is: you can try it out directly onsite and during our excursions in and around Barcelona!

English: we will offer English for intermediate students. You want to get better by speaking with our native speakers and really tune into the english language? Not like in school but here mistakes are allowed and we just really want to get you talking. Join in and have a new fun way to learn English in the beautiful town of Barcelona. Camp language for announcements is English anyways so even there you will pick up many new words!

Course Details: 

  • Duration: 5 days, 3h a day
  • Languages: Spanish beginner & intermediate, Englisch indermediate
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Soccer Camp with C.E.Vila Olímpica

Welcome to the ultimate football experience! Join us for a thrilling 5-day football camp hosted by the C.E.Vila Olímpica. Immerse yourself in the world of football excellence as you embark on a journey of skill development, teamwork, and unforgettable memories.

Camp Highlights:

World-Class Coaching: Our camp brings together top-notch coaches from C.E.Vila Olímpica, providing campers with expert guidance to enhance their football skills. Whether you're a beginner or an aspiring pro, our coaching staff is dedicated to helping you reach your full potential.

State-of-the-Art Facilities: Train like a professional in the first-class facilities of C.E.Vila Olímpica. Campers will have access to cutting-edge training grounds, ensuring an optimal environment for skill refinement and overall player development.

Comprehensive Training Sessions: Each day, campers will participate in dynamic and engaging training sessions designed to improve technical skills, tactical understanding, and overall game awareness. From precision passing to strategic positioning, every aspect of the beautiful game will be covered.

Team Building and Friendship: Football is not just about the game; it's also about building lasting friendships and strong team bonds. Campers will have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for football, fostering a sense of camaraderie both on and off the field.

Course Details: 

  • Duration: 5 days, 3h each day
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Scuba Diving Course

This scuba diving course will introduce you to the fascinating world of diving. Learn the fundamentals of scuba diving at the renowned DIVE CENTER BARCELONA and embark on four unforgettable days that will equip you with the necessary skills to safely explore the underwater realm.

Theory Classes: While still at home, embark on a journey of essential concepts and safety aspects of diving in our online intensive theory classes. From equipment to diving theory, you will be thoroughly prepared. 

Theoretical Examination: Demonstrate your understanding of the theory by completing a theoretical examination and showcasing your knowledge.

Practical Exercises in Confined Water: Once at camp, experience your first breaths underwater in two exciting practical sessions in confined water. Under the guidance of experienced diving instructors, you will learn fundamental skills and techniques.

Open Water Dives: Immerse yourself in the depths of the ocean and apply what you've learned in breathtaking open water dives. This is your opportunity to refine your skills and discover the beauty of the underwater world.

Our certified team of diving instructors is ready to accompany you on this thrilling diving adventure with passion and expertise. If completed successfully, you will be handed the Open Water PADI certification by the end of the week.

Course Details: 

  • Duration: 4 Days
    2 theorie classes, each of them 2h long
    2 practices in contained water, each of them 2h long
    4 open water dives 
  • Participants' Age: 12 - 16 years

Sailing Course – 4 Days Nautical Sailing Adventure

Ahoy, future sailors! Welcome to our thrilling 4-day sailing adventure course designed specifically for teenagers aged 10 to 16. Get ready for an experience that will introduce you to the art of sailing.

From knots and nautical basics to navigation, man-overboard exercises, safety drills, and teamwork, you will be equipped with all the essential skills to sail safely and enjoyably.

Learn from certified sailing instructors who will prepare you for every sailing adventure with passion for teaching and ample experience.

Course details: 

  • Duration: 4 days, 3h a day
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Windsurfing Course – 4 Days Windsurfing Adventure

Dive into the fascinating world of windsurfing.

From the basics of windsurfing to understanding wind directions and mastering various maneuvers, you will learn everything to conquer the water. Our certified windsurfing instructors will prepare you for every windsurfing adventure with their enthusiasm and expertise.

Experience four action-packed days where you not only master the art of windsurfing but also make new friends and share unforgettable moments on the water. Set the sail – or rather, the sail and the mast – for your passion for windsurfing and enjoy 4 days full of fun, action, and new challenges.

Course details: 

  • Duration: 4 days, 3h a day
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Multi-Adventure Water Course – 4 Days Water Adventure

Can't decide whether you prefer sailing or windsurfing, and you'd like to try even more? Then the Multi-Adventure Water Course is right for you.

For 4 days, you'll have the opportunity to try various water sports and maybe discover your new passion.

With other water enthusiasts, spend time on the water and have the chance to try sailing, windsurfing, sea kayaking, and stand-up paddleboarding.

Course details: 

  • Duration: 4 days, 3h a day
  • Participants' Age: 10 - 16 years

Explore the Wonders of Barcelona – A Barcelona Beach Camp Highlight!

As part of our camp, a visit to the enchanting city of Barcelona is undoubtedly a significant highlight. Immerse yourself in the architectural wonders of Antoni Gaudi, stroll along the iconic La Rambla, and experience the breathtaking beauty of the historic Gothic Quarter. Barcelona is a vibrant tapestry of culture, art, and history, making it a superb addition to your adventure. This excursion not only provides a well-deserved break from the sea but also offers a unique opportunity to feel the soul of Catalonia.

Barcelona – where every corner tells a story, and every moment is special. Join us and discover the magic!

Camp Counsellors

Camp Adventure teamers are young people from various countries who have been specially trained to work with children and teenagers. All of them have a valid first aid certification and some hold a Life-Guard certificate.

Each camp will be led by an experienced camp director, who ensures safety and variety in the programme. Due to a good mix of 'old-timers' and newbies with different backgrounds, each camp is unique and so there is never a dull moment for returnee-campers.

The teamers’ rooms are located in between the rooms of the participants, so that they are available 24/7 and can answer questions or help with any problems.

What is included?

Join us and experience the ultimate watersports and language adventure in the heart of Barcelona! Our camp is open to you for 7 or 14 days, whether you decide to take an additional course or not. Immerse yourself in one or two weeks full of exciting experiences, new friendships, and the charm of Barcelona.

We offer you:

Accommodation: Comfortable and secure lodging at the Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC), meeting the high standards of Camp Adventure accommodations.

Meals: Look forward to three nutritious and delicious meals daily, carefully prepared to support your adventure. Special dietary needs? We've got you covered – just let us know in advance. Breakfast and Dinner will be served from the catering team of the sailing center. Lunch - due to the heat - will be light and sandwiches/fruit. Every afternoon we will offer as well a snack.

Excursions: Immerse yourself in the charm of Barcelona. With a guided sightseeing tour, explore the history, architecture, and vibrant atmosphere of the city. Of course, there's still plenty of time to grab a souvenir or two.

24/7 Supervision: Feel secure with our round-the-clock supervision by our experienced and dedicated staff, prioritizing the well-being and safety of every participant.

Activities: We offer various activities, including team-building exercises and group activities, fostering friendships and camaraderie among fellow campers.

Camp T-Shirt: Take home a piece of the adventure – a camp T-shirt that will remind you of the Camp Adventure experience.
