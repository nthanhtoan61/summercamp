Multi Water Adventure in Barcelona – Your Ultimate Watersports Camp!

Dive into endless water fun and explore Barcelona from the water! At Multi Water Adventure Camp, you’ll experience sailing, windsurfing, kayaking, and stand-up paddling.

Perfect for beginners and advanced alike! Camp Adventure partners with the renowned Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC), offering state-of-the-art equipment, top-notch coaches, and the ideal setting for your watersport adventure.

Join an international community and enjoy watersports without language barriers! Our bilingual camp lets you experience action, nature, and team spirit—all in English and Spanish.

Jump into adventure—your watersport holidays 2025 are waiting!

    At a glance

    • Multi Water Adventure – 4 days, 3 hours each day
    • For all levels: beginners & advanced / Age group: 10-16
    • Certified watersport instructors for sailing, windsurfing, and more
    • Perfect conditions at the renowned Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC) with top equipment
    • Activity program (day/evening): outdoor activities, sports, creativity, campfire, disco, and more
    • Cultural experience in Barcelona – weekly trips to the city center and sights
    • Camp languages: English/Spanish – learn languages naturally in an international atmosphere
    • Accommodation: great shared rooms with sea views
    • Full board for 7 days (Sun-Sat), 6 nights, freshly prepared meals
    • 24-hour all-around care: our team is always there for you
    • Digital detox approach: phones and electronic devices allowed only during siesta
    • Arrival & departure services: shuttle transfers from the airport

    Arrival & departure

    All Roads Lead to Camp!

    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle, your child will arrive safely and comfortably. We take care of everything to ensure a smooth journey!

    • Self-Arrival: Simply drop off and pick up your child yourself. You'll receive detailed directions via email six weeks before camp starts, so your arrival will be stress-free.
    • Flight Arrival with Supervision: Our team members accompany all group flights, ensuring a safe arrival and smooth return trip. If a group flight doesn’t happen, we’ll inform you early — allowing free rebooking or cancellation of the camp stay.
    • Shuttle Service: Since our language camps are often in remote areas, we gladly offer shuttle service from the nearby Barcelona airport. Shuttles run only on Sundays for arrival and Saturdays for departure within set time windows. Outside these windows, you can book a private shuttle for an additional fee. Please contact us for arrangements.

    Select your transfer options during the booking process.

    Insurance

    Well protected for your adventure!

    Whether small mishaps or unexpected incidents—our Camp Adventure insurance package has you fully covered.

    Camp Adventure Insurance Package:
    Liability, accident, and health insurance for the entire trip duration.
    ✔ Coverage for accidents & doctor visits
    ✔ Protection against property damage
    💰 Price: from €45 per person per trip
    📅 Valid from arrival to departure
    🌍 Only for trips abroad
    ⚠ Important note: Without valid international insurance, medical costs must be covered privately. We recommend carrying a safety reserve of €500 in cash or a credit card. Participants outside the EU must provide proof of their own insurance before departure.

    Camp Adventure Trip Cancellation Guarantee:
    Our guarantee against homesickness, illness & more. Need to cancel your trip? No problem! With our cancellation guarantee, you get the full Camp Adventure trip price refunded—minus a deductible of €50 (Germany) or €100 (England/Spain).
    ✔ Valid up to one day before trip start
    ✔ Valid regardless of reason: illness, accident, exams, or homesickness
    💰 Price: €45 (for a one-week stay) / €85 (for longer stays)

    All insurance options can be selected during the booking process.

    Booking information

    Multi Water – Your Ultimate Watersport Adventure! 🌊🚣🏄

    Our Multi Water program is designed for one week—perfect for trying out various watersports and experiencing the water element in all its forms!

    All dates are available in the booking area, with Multi Water already preselected. Just complete your booking and secure your spot for Summer Holidays.

    And if you want to explore even more, stay an extra week and try another exciting profile!

    Welcome to Barcelona – Your Beach Camp 2025!

    Our Spain camp at the Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC) is located right on the Mediterranean coast. As a well-established watersports institution, BISC offers the perfect blend of watersports and language learning for 2025!

    Living culture

    Here, you’re right in the middle of it all — BISC isn’t a closed-off camp but a meeting place for locals and campers alike. Nestled in the outskirts of Barcelona, you’ll dive into the vibrant Spanish lifestyle while exploring the city’s top sights and effortlessly switching between English and Spanish.

    Dive in, experience, and open new doors — your language and activity adventure awaits! Come into camp!

    Accommodation at BISC – Our Camp Vibes by the Sea!

    The BISC (Barcelona International Sailing Center) offers modern and comfortable accommodation right on the Mediterranean coast. Here, you enjoy not only a top-notch watersports environment but also sea-view rooms that make your time on land just as enjoyable as your adventures on the water.

    You’ll stay in shared rooms with up to 10 people, separated by gender. Bathrooms are located in the hallways.

    Our team members live right next door, in rooms situated between those of the campers. They’re available around the clock to support you with any questions or concerns and to make sure you feel comfortable and at home.

    Multi Water Adventure – Your Ultimate Watersports Experience! 🌊🚀🏄‍♂️

    Four days, three hours each, packed with non-stop action on the water! Want to try as many watersports as possible in one week? This profile is perfect for you! Whether you're a beginner or almost a pro, Barcelona is the ideal spot for all watersport lovers. The Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC), right on the Mediterranean coast, offers top-notch gear and perfect conditions for your activities.

    What you’ll learn:

    ✔ Sailing, windsurfing, kayaking, stand-up paddling & more – explore the wide variety of watersports!
    ✔ Maneuvers & techniques – learn how to move confidently and safely on the water.
    ✔ Team spirit & coordination – watersports are about teamwork, awareness, and syncing perfectly with wind and waves!

    Everything you need will be provided, so you can fully focus on your adventure with our certified instructors! 🚀🔥

    🌊 Mediterranean vibes meet adventure camp!

    After your activity, the fun continues! Our language coaches are sporty, creative, and bring real international energy to camp. The best part? Most are native speakers fluent in English & Spanish — German? Not so much! This means you’ll dive straight into the language and improve your English or Spanish almost effortlessly.

    Experience Barcelona – sports, language & action!

    Whether it’s beach adventure, creative workshops, or skateboarding — there’s something for everyone! Or just jump into the sea anytime, safely supervised by our certified lifeguards. Besides beach, sailing & windsurfing, challenge friends from around the world in urban soccer.

    Curious what’s waiting for you? Check out our weekly schedule and kick off your adventure!

    🌊 Get ready for the ultimate watersport experience and discover Barcelona from the water’s perspective — we can’t wait to welcome you!

    Meals – Food everyone loves!

    At the BISC Restaurant (Barcelona International Sailing Center), you’ll enjoy a varied and balanced menu every day, prepared with fresh, regional ingredients and always with respect for the environment.

    Three delicious meals a day – with advance notice, vegetarian, gluten-free, lactose-free, and other options are available.

    With views of the sea and the lively atmosphere of the watersports center, you’ll share tasty dishes with new friends and recharge for your next adventures on the water and on land.

    Great food, great company & authentic beach camp vibes – it doesn’t get better than this!

    Comprehensive Care Around the Clock!

    Our experienced and caring team members are there for you 24/7 — with fun, good vibes, and always an open ear. Whether you have small questions or big worries, you can turn to them anytime.

    The best part? Our counselors bring genuine camp spirit from all around the world — that’s why we speak both English and German!

    So you naturally immerse yourself in two languages — during sports, around the campfire, or just chatting. With a supervision ratio between 1:7 and 1:10 and our 24-hour all-inclusive care package, you’re always in great hands!
