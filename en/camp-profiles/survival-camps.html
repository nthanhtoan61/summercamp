    Welcome to Walsrode – Your Adventure in the Lüneburger Heide!

    Our Camp Adventure (Academy) in Walsrode is nestled peacefully in the Lüneburg Heath, right between Bremen, Hamburg, and Hanover. Just a short walk away, you’ll find a charming North German village with farms and timber-framed houses – the perfect place for little discoveries.

    Over 150,000 m² of pure adventure! Our spacious campgrounds are divided into three exciting areas: Beach/Water, Survival & Creative. Whether it’s kayaking, bouncing on the water trampoline, building rafts, making fires, or climbing the high ropes course – there’s always action happening here!

    International community & true camp spirit! Our camp is bilingual (English & German) – you’ll meet participants from all over the world and improve your English naturally along the way.

    Whether you’re a language lover, outdoor adventurer, or creative thinker – discover all the profiles & activities that make your camp experience with us truly unique! We look forward to welcoming you!

    Live like in a small village!

    At our international holiday camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between the tent village and adventure lodges – depending on your sense of adventure!
    Tent Village: Six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and lofts offer a true outdoor experience under the stars.
    Adventure Lodges: Cozy cabins with four to eight beds, shelves, and seating areas. (Note: Overnight stays in the lodges incur an additional charge.)

    Our accommodations are separated by gender, so girls and boys sleep in their own tents or cabins. The groups are also divided by age:

    • Junior (7–12 years)
    • Senior (12–15 years)
    • Senior Plus (15–17 years)

    The washrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

    And the best part: Our teamers live right next door and are there for you around the clock!

    Good to know:
    For tents, you’ll need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat. For the fixed accommodations, bring a fitted sheet and either a sleeping bag or bedding, which you can rent if you wish.

    All accommodation options can be selected during the booking process – secure your spot now!

    Here’s what you can expect from the survival program:
    ✔ Practical survival knowledge – fire-making, finding food, and navigating terrain
    ✔ More confidence & independence – push beyond your limits
    ✔ Led by experienced, certified teamers from around the world

    And after that? Pure action in your free time:
    🏹 Sports & movement: archery, BMX, frisbee, and exciting team games
    🧗 Climb high: high ropes course adventures

    Offline and fully present

    Food everyone will enjoy!

    Three meals a day – fresh, varied, and truly delicious! Whether you need vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-free options, we’ve got something for everyone with advance notice.

    Start your morning with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh rolls, cold cuts, fruit, cereal, juices, and tea. Lunch and dinner usually feature hot dishes and a colorful salad bar. Between meals, you can refuel with fresh fruit, an afternoon snack, and plenty of water – perfect for all your adventures!

    And the best part? We always cook fresh! No instant soups, no bland meals – just good food that tastes great and fuels your energy!

    Fully supported all around!

    Our experienced and caring teamers are there for you 24/7 – with fun, good vibes, and always a listening ear. Whether you have small questions or big worries, you can reach out to them anytime.

      Welcome to Regen – Your Adventure in the Bavarian Forest!

      Our Camp Adventure in Regen is located right in the heart of the Bavarian Forest, one of Southern Germany’s most beautiful natural paradises. Surrounded by forests, hills, and crystal-clear rivers, it’s the perfect place for outdoor adventures, creative activities, and language holidays.

      Pure action & nature! Whether it’s canoeing, kayaking, climbing, or team-building – you’ll get the most out of every moment here! In cooperation with the Bavarian State Sports Association, we use top-class sports facilities on-site and nearby for even more sport and fun.

      International community & true camp spirit! Our bilingual teamers (English/German) create an inspiring atmosphere.

      Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, sports fan, or language adventurer – here you’ll experience a camp like no other! We look forward to welcoming you!

      Living in the heart of nature!

      At our international holiday camp in Bavaria, you can choose how you want to sleep: tent village, tiny houses at the campsite, or log cabins & shared rooms at the BLSV site – all depending on your spirit of adventure!

      Tent Village – For those who love the true camp feeling!
      Here, you’ll sleep in six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and lofts.

      Tiny Houses & Cabins – Cozy & practical!
      Each tiny house or cabin offers bunk beds, small lockers, and a small table – space for up to 6 campers.

      Log Cabins & Shared Rooms at the BLSV Sports Camp
      Modern 2- to 6-bed rooms with bunk beds or spacious log cabins with private bathrooms and lounge areas – perfect for groups of up to 12 campers!

      Accommodations are separated by gender and age groups (Junior 7–12 & Senior 12–16 years). Our teamers live right next door and are available around the clock!
      Washrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

      Practical info about accommodations:
      For tent stays, you need a sleeping bag and a sleeping mat. If you stay in a fixed accommodation, please bring a fitted sheet along with either a sleeping bag or full bedding. If you prefer, you can rent bedding directly from us.
