    Your Holidays. Your Spanish. Your Camp Adventure.

    ¡Hola, future explorador! You want to learn Spanish, finally speak with confidence, make new friends, and enjoy holidays full of sunshine, fun, and adventure? Then our Spanish Camp by Camp Adventure is exactly what you’re looking for. And the best part: you can choose between two amazing locations – Barcelona and Walsrode (Lüneburger Heide, Northern Germany) – to create your own perfect Spanish summer camp experience.

    Learn Spanish in Barcelona – The Classic Language Travel Experience

    Our Spanish Classic Camp in Barcelona takes you straight into the heart of Spanish life and culture. This Spanish language camp in Spain combines interactive language training with sunshine, the beach, and pure Mediterranean energy.
    In the mornings, you’ll learn Spanish with native-speaking teachers – dynamic, engaging, and practical lessons designed to make speaking easy and natural. No boring grammar drills or endless memorizing — instead, you’ll practice speaking, listening, and understanding in real-life contexts.

    In the afternoons, the classroom turns into a playground for adventure: beach time, excursions, sports, or creative workshops. Learning Spanish in Barcelona means living the language — you’ll apply what you’ve learned right away, meet international friends, and explore local culture.

    Why Barcelona? Because here you experience Spanish where it’s spoken. You’ll wake up to the sound of the sea, stroll through vibrant city streets in the afternoon, talk to locals and fellow campers, and naturally expand your vocabulary and fluency. In our Spanish language camp in Barcelona, Spain, learning becomes an experience — and Spanish becomes part of your everyday life.

    The language program at the Barcelona Camp is perfect for both beginners and advanced learners. After a short placement test, you’ll join a group that matches your level, focusing on conversation, listening, reading, and grammar. All of this happens in a bilingual environment – Spanish and English – with campers from around the world. You’ll not only dive into the language, but also into an international community full of energy, laughter, and shared experiences.

    Afternoons bring a variety of exciting activities – choose watersports, football, or creative workshops, and use your Spanish naturally throughout the day.

    New: Spanish Camp in Walsrode (Lüneburger Heide, Northern Germany)

    New this year – our Spanish Language Camp in Walsrode! Yes, you heard right: now you can enjoy an amazing Spanish learning experience right here in Germany. Located in the beautiful Lüneburger Heide between Hamburg, Bremen, and Hanover, our Camp Adventure Academy in Walsrode has long been famous for its adventure and sports camps. Now it’s time to add something new – Spanish meets adventure!

    Here you can learn Spanish in Germany while enjoying all the excitement of a traditional summer camp: tents or lodges, outdoor adventures, and an international community. It’s the perfect choice for anyone who wants to learn Spanish without leaving Germany, but still experience that international summer camp feeling.

    At our Walsrode Spanish Classic Camp, mornings are dedicated to interactive Spanish lessons with native-speaking teachers, and afternoons are packed with activities, workshops, and games. Evenings bring campfires, music, and friendships that last a lifetime. It’s a real holiday camp atmosphere – but with a bilingual twist.

    The location itself is an adventure: our Camp Adventure site in Walsrode offers over 150,000 m² of space for fun, creativity, and nature. Stay in our Tent Village or Adventure Lodges, enjoy modern facilities, and benefit from 24-hour supervision. Digital detox, outdoor fun, and real-life connections are part of the experience.

    At this Spanish camp in Germany, language learning goes far beyond the classroom – you’ll use Spanish while climbing, building rafts, practicing survival skills, or sitting around the campfire. Surrounded by nature and international campers, your Spanish becomes part of your daily life – fun, effortless, and truly lasting.

    Why Both Camps Offer the Perfect Spanish Experience

    Whether you choose Barcelona or Walsrode, your Spanish summer camp with Camp Adventure combines structured learning with unforgettable experiences. Both locations share the same values and quality standards:

    • Native-speaking teachers and bilingual coaching teams
    • Small learning groups for maximum progress
    • Dynamic language programs focusing on speaking, listening, reading & grammar
    • Afternoon and evening activities that make Spanish come alive
    • International community – make friends from all over the world
    • 24-hour supervision for safety and comfort
    • Flexible choice – beach & sunshine in Barcelona or nature & adventure in the Lüneburger heide

    A Week at Camp Adventure – What to Expect

    In Barcelona:
    You arrive at the Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC), check in, and meet new friends from around the globe. Every morning, you’ll have Spanish lessons – 20 sessions per week à 45 minutes – with native teachers who make learning fun and interactive. You’ll practice real conversation and gain confidence every single day.

    Afternoons are all about exploring: beach time, surfing, beach volleyball, city tours, or creative workshops. Evenings bring campfires, discos, or relaxed hangouts by the harbor. You’ll switch between Spanish, English, and maybe even some German as you make friends, share stories, and grow more fluent every day.

    In Walsrode:
    You might arrive by train or car and step into a forest-filled adventure site surrounded by nature. From the first day, you’re part of an international group full of energy and excitement.
    Mornings bring interactive, level-based Spanish lessons — fun, active, and engaging. Afternoons are for your chosen profile: Spanish, sports, creative workshops, or survival training. You’ll pitch your tent or move into your lodge, spend your days outdoors, and use your Spanish naturally while climbing, building, or exploring.
    Evenings are for connection: campfires, music, laughter, and that unique Camp Adventure community spirit that turns a week into an unforgettable experience. Every day, your Spanish improves — and so does your confidence.

    Why You’ll Make Faster Progress at Camp Adventure

    Because Camp Adventure isn’t a classroom — it’s a way of life.
    We don’t teach languages through textbooks, we live them. Whether you’re in Barcelona or Walsrode, Spanish becomes part of your daily routine. You’ll speak during sports, at mealtimes, on excursions, and at evening events. Your teachers are passionate native speakers who make every class interactive and alive.

    With small groups and a motivating, international atmosphere, you’ll make visible progress in just days. Learning Spanish becomes fun, practical, and unforgettable — exactly the way it should be.

    Booking & Important Information

    Booking your Spanish Camp 2025 is easy: choose your preferred location, fill in your details, and secure your spot!

    In Barcelona:
    You’ll stay at the Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC) with full board, 24-hour supervision, an exciting activity program, and your complete Spanish course included.

    In Walsrode:
    You’ll stay in the Tent Village or Adventure Lodges, enjoy full board, international participants, Spanish lessons, and our adventure activity program.

    Prices and dates are available in the booking section. Both options – the Spanish Camp in Germany (Walsrode) and the Spanish Camp in Barcelona (Spain) – can be booked directly online.

    ⚠ Please note: places are limited and supervision is intensive, so early booking is highly recommended!

    Why Camp Adventure Is Your Perfect Partner

    At Camp Adventure, quality comes first.
    We offer small groups, trained counselors, our own camp facilities, structured offline time (Digital Detox), and a welcoming international atmosphere. Our campers love it so much that many come back year after year – as Seniors, Teamers, or part of our Leadership Program.

    Both locations, Barcelona and Walsrode, share the same mission: language, adventure, and community.

    Conclusion

    Whether you prefer sunshine and sea or nature and adventure, our Spanish Camps by Camp Adventure offer you a truly unique mix of language learning and holiday fun. Learn Spanish with joy and success – in Barcelona or in Walsrode, Germany.

    You’ll grow your vocabulary, boost your confidence, make lifelong friends, and return home with memories that stay forever.

    Spanish Camp Barcelona, Spain or Spanish Camp Walsrode, Germany – and turn your holidays into a real language journey full of sun, fun, and Spanish!
