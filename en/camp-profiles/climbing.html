Climbing Camp – Your Adventure in Bavaria!

Step by Step, Reach New Heights!
At our Climbing Camp in Southern Germany, you’ll find the perfect mix of fun, challenge, and team spirit — whether you’re a beginner or already have some experience. Practice top-rope climbing and try out eight exciting routes with varying difficulty levels. And of course, you’ll always have a strong team by your side — because safety comes first!

Dare to push your limits and gain the kind of confidence that helps you overcome challenges not only on the wall, but also in everyday life!

    Everything you need to know

    • 3-Day Climbing Course, 3 Hours Each Day
      For all levels: beginners and experienced climbers welcome
    • Full-Day Diverse Program: Community & adventure activities including canoeing, archery, climbing, and swimming pool fun
    • Colorful Evening Program: Campfires, stick bread, Capture the Flag, and more
    • Cool Trips & Excursions for Two-Week Stays: Outdoor overnight hike or a city trip to Munich
    • Camp Language: English/German — Learn languages playfully in an international atmosphere
    • Full Board for 7 Days (Sun–Sat), 6 Nights: Freshly prepared meals
    • 24/7 All-Round Care: Our team leaders are always there for you
    • Digital Detox Approach: Phones and electronic devices are allowed only during siesta time

    Arrival & Departure

    All Roads Lead to Camp!
    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle — your child will arrive safely and comfortably. We take care to make sure everything runs smoothly!

    • Self-arrival: Simply drop off and pick up yourself. You’ll receive detailed directions six weeks before camp starts via info email — so nothing stands in the way of a relaxed arrival.
    • Shuttle service: Since our adventure camps are often in remote areas, we’re happy to shuttle you from nearby airports (Munich) and train stations (Plattling).

    The shuttles run only on Sundays for arrival and Saturdays for departure within set time windows. On these days, outside the designated time slots, you can book a private shuttle for an extra fee. Please contact us individually for this.

    Munich Airport
    Arrival (landing): 10:00 – 15:00

    Departure (take-off): 12:00 – 16:00
    Meeting point (McDonald's): 10:00

    Plattling Train Station
    Arrival: 14:00 – 16:00
    Meeting point (platform 5): 15:30
    Departure: 10:00 – 12:00
    Meeting point (platform 5): 9:30

    Additional Booking information - Climbing Camp

    Climbing – Reach New Heights! 🧗‍♂️🏔️

    Our climbing program lasts one week – perfect for conquering new heights and testing your limits!

    All dates are available in the booking section, and climbing is already pre-selected. Just add your preferred accommodation, arrival/departure options, and more – secure your spot now and dive into your next adventure!

    And if you want to explore even more: stay an extra week and try out another exciting program!

    Welcome to Regen – Your Adventure in the Bavarian Forest!
    Our Camp Adventure in Regen is located right in the heart of the Bavarian Forest, one of Southern Germany’s most beautiful natural paradises. Surrounded by forests, hills, and crystal-clear rivers, it’s the perfect place for outdoor adventures, creative activities, and language holidays.

    Pure action and nature! Whether it’s canoeing & kayaking, climbing, or team building — here you’ll get your fill of excitement! In cooperation with the Bavarian State Sports Association, we use top-notch sports facilities on site and nearby for even more sport and fun.

    International community & camp spirit! Our bilingual team leaders (English/German) create an inspiring atmosphere.

    Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, sports fanatic, or language adventurer — here you’ll experience a camp like no other! We’re excited to welcome you!

    Stay Right in the Heart of Nature!
    At our international holiday camp in Bavaria, you can choose how you want to sleep: Tent Village, Tiny Houses at the campsite, or log cabins & shared rooms at the BLSV grounds — whatever suits your sense of adventure!

    Tent Village – For those who love real camp vibes!
    Sleep in six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and lofts.

    Tiny Houses & Cabins – Cozy & practical!
    Each tiny house or cabin offers bunk beds, small wardrobes, and a little table — space for up to 6 campers.

    Log Cabins & Shared Rooms at the BLSV Sports Camp
    Modern rooms for 2 to 6 people with bunk beds or spacious log cabins with private bathrooms and lounges — perfect for groups of up to 12 campers!

    Accommodations are separated by gender and age groups (Junior 7–12 & Senior 12–16 years). Our team leaders live right next door and are available around the clock!
    Bathrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

    Good to know:
    For tents, you’ll need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat; for the solid accommodations, bring a fitted sheet with your sleeping bag or bedding — which you can also rent if you like.

    Loads of Adventure!
    The climbing course takes place four days a week, three hours each morning. Here are just a few of the highlights waiting for you:

    • A challenging climbing wall: Eight routes with varying difficulty levels — overhangs included!
    • Rope and knot skills: Terms like dynamic vs. static ropes or double figure-eight knots won’t be foreign to you after the course.
    • Professional instruction: Led by certified expert instructors from the Bavarian State Sports Association.

    Our different profiles run in parallel, so each week you can choose one main program to focus on.

    Outside of profile times, you can pick from a variety of exciting activities — there’s always something new to discover, depending on the weather and offerings! Look forward to highlights like:

    • Water action: Outdoor pool, kayaking, and building giant rafts
    • Survival training: Making fire, building shelters, and learning how to survive in nature
    • Creative expression: Exciting workshops for all who want to unleash their artistic side

    And in the evening? Campfires, talent shows, and group activities make the perfect end to an eventful day!

    Food Everyone Loves!
    Three meals a day — fresh, varied, and really delicious! Whether vegetarian, gluten- or lactose-free (with advance notice), there’s something for everyone.

    Start your mornings with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh rolls, cold cuts, fruit, muesli, juices, and tea. For lunch and dinner, you’ll usually get warm dishes and a colorful salad bar. In between, you can refuel with fresh fruit, an afternoon snack, and plenty of water — perfect for all your adventures!

    And the best part? We always cook fresh! No instant soups, no one-size-fits-all meals — just good food that tastes great and gives you energy!

    Fully Supported All Around!
    Our experienced team leaders are there for you 24/7 — with fun, good vibes, and always ready to listen. Whether you have a small question or a bigger concern, you can reach out to them anytime.

    The best part? Our team brings true camp spirit from all over the world — so we speak both English and German! This way, you naturally immerse yourself in two languages — during sports, around the campfire, or just in everyday conversations.

    Our team-to-camper ratio ranges from 1:7 to 1:10. Thanks to our 24-hour all-around-care package, you’re always in the best hands!
