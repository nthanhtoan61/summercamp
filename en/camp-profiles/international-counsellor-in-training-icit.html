        YOUR ICIT TRAINING LOCATION

        To grow, you need the right environment – and you'll find it at the BLSV Sportcamp Bischofsgrün, our trusted partner in Northern Bavaria.

        Located in the southern part of Bischofsgrün, right at the foot of the 1,024 m high Ochsenkopf mountain and nestled in the peaceful nature of the legendary Fichtelgebirge range, the camp is just 30 minutes from Bayreuth. It's the ideal place for active learning, personal development, and fresh mountain air.

        With over 55,000 m² of space, the sport camp offers the perfect setting for team building, outdoor challenges, and leadership growth.         Many of our trainers come from abroad, bringing authentic international camp spirit with them. English is naturally part of everyday life at camp and serves as the main training language. This way, you’ll pick up intercultural soft skills simply by being part of the experience.

        As one of the oldest age groups (17/18 years), you’ll already take on real responsibility – for yourself and for others. And don’t worry: we’ll be there to support you every step of the way – with trust, encouragement, and a whole lot of team spirit.
