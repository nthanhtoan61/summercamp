International Camp in the Lüneburg Heath -
Show us what you got!

Are you ready to take on new challenges, push your limits, and take real responsibility?
At our international Leadership Camp, you’ll meet peers from all over the world, develop your leadership skills – and do it all in English… just like in real life!

For the Senior Plus age group (15–17 years):
Here, you'll learn how to make decisions, lead teams, and explore new paths. Whether you're taking on exciting outdoor challenges or diving into inspiring workshops – you’ll discover what you’re truly capable of!

Connect with the leaders of tomorrow and start building an international network that will stay with you long after camp ends.

Step up to the challenge – and become the leader of tomorrow!

    Everything you need to know

    • 2-Week Camp Experience (Sun–Sat)
    • Exclusive Age Group: For Senior Plus only (ages 15–17) – real challenges for tomorrow’s leaders.
    • English-Language Program: A wide range of workshops on topics like team leadership and problem-solving.
    • Personal Growth: Push your limits, unlock your potential, and grow beyond what you thought possible.
    • Adventure Highlight: A 3-day overnight hike all the way to the North Sea – packed with challenges and teamwork.
    • Clear Philosophy: “No drugs, no alcohol – just fun!”
    • Exciting Evening Program: Campfires, marshmallow roasting, Capture the Flag, and more.
    • Full Board: Freshly prepared meals throughout the entire camp stay.
    • 24/7 Support: Our trained teamers are always there for you.
    • Digital Detox Approach: Phones and electronic devices are allowed only during siesta time.

    Arrival & Departure

    All Roads Lead to Camp!

    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle – your child will travel safely and stress-free. We’ll make sure everything runs smoothly!

    🚗 Arrival by Car
    You’re welcome to drop off and pick up your child yourself. A detailed route description will be sent to you via email 6 weeks before camp starts – so nothing stands in the way of a relaxed arrival.

    🚌 Shuttle Service
    Since many of our adventure camps are located in more remote areas, we’re happy to provide shuttle services from nearby airports and train stations.
    Shuttles operate only on Sundays (arrival) and Saturdays (departure) within set time slots.

    Need a transfer outside the official time slots? No problem – you can book a private shuttle for an extra fee. Please contact us directly for individual arrangements.

    Insurance

    Well Covered on Your Camp Adventure!
    Whether it’s a minor mishap or an unexpected incident – with our Camp Adventure Insurance Package, you’re fully protected throughout your journey.

    🛡️ Camp Adventure Insurance Package:
    Includes liability, accident, and health insurance for the entire duration of your trip.

    ✔ Coverage for accidents & doctor visits
    ✔ Protection for damages to property
    💰 Price: from €45 per person per trip
    📅 Valid from departure to return
    🌍 Available only for trips abroad

    ⚠ Important:
    Without international health insurance coverage, any medical costs must be paid by the participant. We recommend carrying a safety reserve of €500 in cash or a credit card.
    Participants from outside the EU must provide proof of their own insurance before the trip begins.

    🔁 Camp Adventure Trip Cancellation Guarantee
    Our safety net in case of homesickness, illness, or other last-minute changes. Need to cancel your trip? No worries! With our Cancellation Guarantee, you’ll receive a full refund of your camp fee – minus a deductible of €50 (Germany) or €100 (UK/Spain).

    ✔ Valid up to one day before departure
    ✔ Covers all reasons – illness, injury, exams, or homesickness
    💰 Price: €45 (1-week stay) / €85 (multi-week stay)

    🛒 You can easily select all insurance options during the booking process.

    Additional Booking Information - Leadership Program

    Book Your Leadership Experience
    Our Leadership profile is designed for a two-week camp stay (Sun–Sat) – the perfect setting to dive deep into the world of leadership.

    Secure your spot now and kick off your one-of-a-kind Leadership Adventure!

    Welcome to Walsrode – Your Adventure in the Lüneburger Heide!

    Our Camp Adventure Academy in Walsrode is set in the beautiful Lüneburger Heide, perfectly located between Bremen, Hamburg, and Hanover. Just a short walk away, you’ll find a charming North German village with traditional farmhouses and timber-framed buildings – the ideal place for little discoveries.

    Over 150,000 m² of pure adventure!
    Our spacious camp is divided into three exciting zones: Beach & Water, Survival, and Creative. From kayaking, water trampolining, and raft building to fire-making and high ropes climbing – there's always something going on!

    International community & real camp spirit!
    Our camp is bilingual (English & German), welcoming campers from around the world – the perfect opportunity to boost your English skills without even noticing.

    Whether you’re a language lover, outdoor adventurer, or creative mind – explore all our profiles & activities and make your camp experience truly unforgettable.

    We can’t wait to meet you!

    Live Like in a Little Village!
    At our international holiday camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between Tent Village and Adventure Lodges — depending on your sense of adventure!

    Tent Village: Six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and lofts offer a true outdoor experience under the stars.
    Adventure Lodges: Cozy cabins with four to eight beds, shelves, and seating areas. (Please note: staying in the lodges comes with an additional cost.)

    Our accommodations are separated by gender, so girls and boys sleep in separate tents or cabins. The groups are also divided by age:

    Junior (7–12 years)

    Senior (12–15 years)

    Senior Plus (15–17 years)

    Bathrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

    And the best part? Our team leaders live right next door and are available for you around the clock!

    Good to know:
    For tents, you’ll need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat. For the solid accommodations, bring a fitted sheet along with your sleeping bag or bedding — which you can also rent if you want.

    All accommodation options can be selected during the booking process — secure your spot today!

    Your Challenge Awaits!
    Experience our exclusive Leadership Camp, designed especially for our Senior Plus age group (15–17 years)! Over two weeks (Sun–Sat), you’ll dive into an intensive program with 8 sessions of 2.5 hours each focused on leadership. From self-reflection and team management to problem-solving, you’ll gain all the essential tools every future leader needs.

    The highlight? A three-day leadership hike to the North Sea, where you’ll push your limits and tackle real challenges.

    But that’s not all: in the afternoons, you can choose from a variety of exciting activities. Evenings are all about cozy campfires, talent shows, and fun group events – the perfect way to end an adventurous day.

    Pack your bags and get ready for an experience that will challenge you and help you grow beyond yourself!

    Food Everyone Will Enjoy!
    Three meals a day — fresh, varied, and really delicious! Whether you need vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-free options (with prior notice), there’s something for everyone.

    Start your mornings with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh bread rolls, cold cuts, fruit, muesli, juices, and tea. For lunch and dinner, you’ll usually get warm dishes along with a colorful salad bar. Throughout the day, you can recharge with fresh fruit, an afternoon snack, and plenty of water — perfect fuel for all your adventures!

    And the best part? We always cook fresh! No instant soups, no one-size-fits-all meals — just good food that tastes great and gives you energy!

    Fully Supported All Around!
    Our experienced team leaders are there for you 24/7 — bringing fun, good vibes, and always ready to listen. Whether it’s a small question or a bigger concern, you can reach out to them anytime.

    The best part? Our team brings the true camp spirit from all over the world — that’s why we speak English! All leadership-related activities take place in both English and German.

    As a Senior Plus camper (ages 15–17), you don’t just hold the title of the “big kids” in camp — you also take on responsibility, for yourself and your fellow campers. But don’t worry — we’ll be right by your side throughout this once-in-a-lifetime adventure.
