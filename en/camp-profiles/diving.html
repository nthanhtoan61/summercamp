Suba Dive Camp Barcelona – Your Underwater Adventure!

Dive in, go deep, and be amazed!
At our Dive Camp in Barcelona, you'll explore the fascinating underwater world of the Mediterranean and learn everything you need for safe diving – in both English and Spanish!

Learn to dive with the pros!
In cooperation with Dive Center Barcelona, experienced instructors will guide you step by step – whether you're a beginner or already have some scuba diving experience. By the end, you’ll have the chance to earn your internationally recognized! CMAS 1 Star Diver certification, allowing you to dive up to 25 meters deep!

Dive into a whole new world with like-minded adventurers, build team spirit, and experience the ultimate summer fun – both above and below the water!

    All you need to know

    • Scuba diving – 4 Days × 3 Hours
    • For all levels: Beginners & advanced / Age group: 10–16 years
    • Certified dive instructors through our partnership with Dive Center Barcelona
    • CMAS 1 Star Diver certification – internationally recognized & allows dives up to 25 meters deep! Theory/Test in English
    • Activity program (day & evening): Outdoor fun, sports, creativity, campfires, disco nights & more
    • Cultural experience in Barcelona: Weekly trip to the city center & famous sights
    • Camp languages: English/Spanish – Learn naturally in an international atmosphere
    • Accommodation: Comfortable shared rooms with sea views
    • Full board for 7 days (Sun–Sat), 6 nights – with freshly prepared meals
    • 24/7 all-inclusive care: Our teamers are always there for you
    • Digital detox approach: Phones and devices only during siesta
    • Arrival & departure service: Shuttle transfers available from the airport

    Arrival and Departure

    All roads lead to camp

    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle – your child will travel safely and stress-free. We make sure everything runs smoothly!

    • Own arrival: Simply drop off and pick up your child yourself. You'll receive detailed directions via info email about six weeks before the camp starts – so nothing stands in the way of a relaxed arrival.
    • Flight with supervision: Our teamers accompany all group flights, ensure a safe arrival, and make sure everything goes smoothly on the way back as well. If a group flight doesn’t go ahead, we’ll inform you in advance – free rebooking or cancellation of the camp stay is then possible.
    • Shuttle service: Since our language camps are usually located in remote areas, we’re happy to offer shuttle transfers from the nearby airport, Barcelona.

    Shuttles run only on Sundays for arrival and Saturdays for departure within set time windows. Outside these times, you can book a private shuttle for an extra fee. Just contact us directly.

    You can choose your transfer option during the booking process.

    Insurance

    Well insured for your adventure

    Whether it’s small mishaps or unexpected incidents – with our Camp Adventure insurance, you’re fully covered throughout your stay.

    The Camp Adventure Insurance Package:
    Liability, accident, and health insurance for the entire trip
    ✔ Coverage for accidents and medical visits
    ✔ Protection for damage to property
    Price: from €45 per person per trip
    Valid from arrival to departure
    Only for international trips

    Important note: Without international health insurance, medical costs must be paid out of pocket. We recommend bringing a cash reserve of €500 or a credit card. Participants from outside the EU must provide proof of their own insurance before departure.

    The Camp Adventure Trip Cancellation Guarantee:
    Our safety net for homesickness, illness & more. Need to cancel? No problem! With our cancellation guarantee, you'll get a full refund of your Camp Adventure booking – minus a deductible of €50 (Germany) or €100 (UK/Spain).
    ✔ Valid up to one day before departure
    ✔ No matter the reason: illness, accident, exams, or homesickness
    Price: €45 (for one-week stays) / €85 (for multi-week stays)

    You can select all insurance options during the booking process.

    Your summer holidays with Camp Adventure in Barcelona! 🇪🇸☀️

    Welcome to Barcelona – Your Beach Camp 2025!

    Our Spain camp at the Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC) is located right on the Mediterranean coast. As a renowned watersports hub, BISC offers the perfect mix of watersports and language travel for your 2025 adventure!

    A True Cultural Experience
    Here, you’re right in the middle of it all – BISC isn’t an isolated camp, but a vibrant meeting place for locals and international campers alike. Nestled on the edge of Barcelona, you’ll dive into the heartbeat of Spanish life while exploring top sights and naturally switching between English and Spanish.
    Dive in, explore, and open new doors – your language & activity adventure awaits! Come into Camp!

    Accommodation at BISC – Our Beachside Camp Vibe

    The Barcelona International Sailing Center (BISC) offers modern, comfortable accommodations right on the Mediterranean coast. Here, you’ll enjoy not only a top-notch watersports setting but also sea-view rooms that make your time on land just as enjoyable as your adventures in the water.

    You’ll stay in shared rooms for up to 10 people, separated by gender. Restrooms are located on each floor.

    Our teamers live right next door, in rooms between those of the participants. They’re available for you 24/7, ready to help with questions, concerns, or anything else you might need to feel at home.

    Scuba diving – Your Underwater Adventure! 🤿🌊🐠

    For four days, 3 hours each, it’s all about diving. Whether you’re a beginner or already have some experience – here, you’ll become a certified CMAS 1 Star Diver step by step, allowing you to dive up to 25 meters deep in the future!

    Course highlights:

    ✔ Online theory course in advance – Learn the diving basics from home in 3–4 hours.
    ✔ First dives in sheltered waters – Get familiar with the gear and practice the fundamentals.
    ✔ Off into the open sea – Explore the fascinating underwater world in 6 exciting dives.
    ✔ CMAS 1 Star Diver Certification – Your internationally recognized ticket to 25-meter dives.

    All equipment is provided – so you can fully focus on your adventure. With our partner Dive Center Barcelona, you’ll learn to master the world of diving.

    Mediterranean coast meets activity camp!

    After your booked activity, the adventure continues! Our language coaches are athletic, creative, and bring a real international vibe to camp. The best part? Most are native speakers of English and Spanish – no German here! That’s what makes the difference: you’ll be fully immersed in the language and improve your English or Spanish almost effortlessly.

    Experience Barcelona – sports, language, and action!

    From beach adventures and creative workshops to skateboarding – there’s something for everyone! Want to jump into the sea? Go for it – always under the supervision of our certified lifeguards. In addition to the beach, sailing, and windsurfing, you can also test your skills in urban soccer with friends from around the world.

    Curious about what’s waiting for you? Check out our weekly schedule and start your adventure!

    We can’t wait to see you!
    Your Camp Adventure Team

    Meals – Food Everyone Will Enjoy!

    At the BISC restaurant (Barcelona International Sailing Center), you'll enjoy a varied and balanced diet every day – made with fresh, local ingredients and always with sustainability in mind.

    Three tasty meals a day – vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-free options available with advance notice.

    With a sea view and the vibrant atmosphere of the watersports center, you’ll enjoy delicious meals with new friends and recharge for your next adventures on and off the water.

    Great food, great company, and real beach camp vibes – it doesn’t get better than this!

    Well cared for – all day, every day

    Our experienced and caring teamers are there for you around the clock – with positive energy, good vibes, and always a listening ear. Whether it’s a small question or a big concern, you can count on them anytime.

    The best part? Our staff bring real camp spirit from all over the world – which is why we speak both English and German!

    This way, you'll naturally immerse yourself in both languages – during sports, around the campfire, or just chatting. Our supervision ratio is between 1:7 and 1:10. Thanks to our 24/7 all-round care package, you're always in the best hands!
