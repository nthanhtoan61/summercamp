    Arrival and Departure

    All Roads Lead to Camp!

    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle – your child will arrive safely and stress-free. We make sure everything runs smoothly from start to finish!

    Self-arrival: You’re welcome to bring and pick up your child yourself. About six weeks before camp starts, you’ll receive detailed directions via email – so you can plan your trip without any stress.

    Shuttle service: Since our adventure camps are often located in more remote areas, we offer convenient shuttle transfers from nearby airports and train stations. 
    Shuttles run only on Sundays (arrival) and Saturdays (departure) within fixed time slots. Outside these hours, individual transfers may be arranged for an additional fee – just reach out to us directly.

    Welcome to Walsrode – Your Adventure in the Lüneburger Heide! 

    Our Camp Adventure (Academy) in Walsrode is nestled in the beautiful Lüneburger Heide, right between Bremen, Hamburg, and Hannover. Just a short walk away, you’ll find a charming North German village with traditional farms and timber-framed houses – the perfect setting for unforgettable outdoor, sports, and creative holidays in Northern Germany.

    Over 150,000 m² of pure adventure! 
    Our spacious camp is divided into three exciting zones: Beach & Water, Survival, and Creativity. 
    From kayaking, water trampolining, and raft building to fire-making and climbing in the high ropes course – there's non-stop action here!

    International community & true camp spirit! 
    Our camp is bilingual (English & German) – meet campers from all over the world and improve your English naturally as you go.

    Whether you’re a language enthusiast, an outdoor adventurer, or a creative mind – explore all our focus programs and activities that make your camp experience truly unique.

    We can’t wait to meet you!

    Live Like in a Small Village! 

    At our international holiday camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between our Tent Village and Adventure Lodges – depending on your sense of adventure!

    Tent Village: 
    Six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and a loft provide a true outdoor feeling under the stars.

    Adventure Lodges: 
    Comfortable cabins with four to eight beds, shelving, and seating areas. 
    (Note: Staying in a lodge comes at an additional cost.)

    Our accommodations are separated by gender, so boys and girls sleep in their own tents or lodges. We also group campers by age:

    Junior (7–12 years)
    Senior (12–15 years)
    Senior Plus (15–17 years)
    The washrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

    And the best part: our teamers live right next door and are there for you 24/7!

    Good to know: 
    For the tents, you'll need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat. For the cabins, please bring a fitted sheet and either a sleeping bag or bedding – both can be rented if needed.

    You can choose your preferred accommodation during the booking process – secure your spot now!

    Your Creative Camp!


    For four days, you'll dive into Arts & Crafts for 2.5 hours each – crafting, building, creating: this is where every creative mind feels at home!

    Whether it’s designing jewellery, upcycling projects, or large-scale artworks with watercolour, wood, and stone – our workshops let you unleash your artistic side and try out new techniques. No matter if you already have experience or are just starting to explore the world of art, here it's all about your imagination! 🎨✨

    After your booked activity, the day is just getting started! Three experience zones – endless possibilities: our 150,000 m² camp is divided into Beach/Water, Survival & Creative areas – there's something for everyone:

    💦 Water action: kayak rides, water trampoline fun & raft building
    🧗 Reaching new heights: high ropes climbing
    🔥 Survival training: make fire, build shelters & explore nature
    🎨 Unleash creativity: exciting workshops for everyone wanting to express themselves artistically

    Our international team brings the true camp spirit – full of action, adventure and fun. English becomes a natural part of daily life – during sports, creative projects, or relaxed evenings around the campfire.

    Digital Detox – Experience more, scroll less! Phones are only used during siesta – which means more time for real adventures, new friendships & unforgettable memories.

    Three delicious meals a day – fresh, varied, and full of flavour!

    Whether you’re vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-intolerant – we’ve got you covered. Just let us know in advance, and we’ll make sure there’s something for everyone.

    Your day starts with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh bread rolls, cold cuts, fruit, muesli, juice, and tea. At lunch and dinner, we usually serve hot meals along with a colourful salad bar. And in between? You’ll refuel with fresh fruit, an afternoon snack, and plenty of water – just what you need to keep going strong through all your adventures.

    And the best part? Our food is always freshly cooked! No instant soups, no bland canteen meals – just real, tasty food that keeps you energized and ready for fun.

    Well Taken Care of – All Day, Every Day!

    Our experienced and enthusiastic teamers are there for you around the clock – always with a smile, lots of energy, and a listening ear. Whether it’s a quick question or something more serious, you can count on them at any time.

    And the best part? Our staff brings real camp spirit from all over the world – which is why we speak both English and German!

    That way, you’ll naturally immerse yourself in both languages – while playing sports, sitting around the campfire, or just chatting throughout the day.

    We maintain a supervision ratio of about 1:7 to 1:10. With our all-round 24-hour support, you’re truly in good hands!

      Welcome to Regen – Your Adventure in Southern Germany!

      Our Camp Adventure in Regen is located in the heart of the Bavarian Forest, one of Southern Germany’s most beautiful natural paradises. Surrounded by forests, hills, and crystal-clear rivers, it’s the perfect place for outdoor adventures, creative activities, and language holidays.

      Pure action & nature!

      Whether it’s canoeing, kayaking, climbing, or team-building – you’ll get your money’s worth! In cooperation with the Bavarian State Sports Association, we use top-class sports facilities on site and in the surrounding area for even more sport and fun.

      International community & camp spirit!

      Our bilingual teamers (English/German) create an inspiring atmosphere.

      Whether you're an outdoor fan, sports enthusiast, or language adventurer – this is a top-class camp experience! We’re looking forward to seeing you!

      Living in the Heart of Nature!

      At our international camp in Bavaria, you can choose how you’d like to sleep: tent village, tiny houses at the campsite, or log cabins & shared rooms on the BLSV grounds – wherever your adventurous heart takes you!

      Tent Village – For everyone who loves the real camp feeling!
      Here you’ll sleep in six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and a loft area.

      Tiny Houses & Cabins – Cozy & practical!
      Each tiny house or cabin includes bunk beds, small cupboards, and a small table – with space for up to 6 campers.

      Log Cabins & Shared Rooms at the BLSV Sports Camp
      Modern two- to six-bed rooms with bunk beds, or spacious log cabins with private bathrooms and a lounge area – perfect for groups of up to 12 campers!

      Accommodations are separated by gender and age groups (Junior 7–12 & Senior 12–16 years). Our teamers live right next door and are there for you around the clock!
      The washrooms are also separated by gender and always close by.

      Practical info about the accommodation:
      For the tent overnight stay, you’ll need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat. If you’re staying in solid housing, please bring a fitted sheet – plus either a sleeping bag or complete bedding. If you prefer, you can also rent bedding directly from us.

      Arts & Crafts – Your Creativity Camp in Regen!


      For four days, 2.5 hours each, you’ll get to craft, build & create – a place where creative minds can bring their ideas to life!

      Whether it’s designing jewelry, upcycling projects, or making artwork with watercolor, wood, or stone – in our workshops, you’ll try out new techniques and let your imagination run wild. No prior experience needed – here, it’s all about your creativity! 🎨✨

      Creativity meets nature!
      Right in the middle of the Bavarian Forest, you’ll find inspiration in your surroundings and develop your talent – using natural materials, colors & creative techniques. In addition to the arts & crafts program, exciting activities await you:

      English comes naturally!
      Our team from around the world brings the real camp spirit! English becomes a natural part of everyday life – in the workshops, during outdoor activities, or while relaxing by the campfire in the evening.

      Let your creativity flow & enjoy an unforgettable holiday in Regen!
