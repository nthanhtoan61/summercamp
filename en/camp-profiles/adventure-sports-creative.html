Adventure Camp Lüneburger Heide – An Action-Packed Summer 2026!
Make international friends, explore nature, and discover new talents! Our Adventure Camp in the Lüneburger Heide is the perfect choice for kids and teens who love movement, creativity, and outdoor fun.

Climbing, team challenges, sports, and creative workshops – this well-rounded base program is included in every camp without a specific focus like horseback riding or survival training. It’s the ideal option for anyone who wants a bit of everything!

Most campers fall in love at first sight – will you be next?

      Welcome to Walsrode – Your Adventure in the Lüneburger Heide!

      Our Camp Adventure (Academy) in Walsrode is set in the beautiful Lüneburger Heide, right between Bremen, Hamburg, and Hanover. Just a short walk away, you’ll find a charming northern German village with traditional farms and timber-framed houses – the perfect setting for an unforgettable mix of adventure, sports, and creativity in northern Germany.

      Over 150,000 m² of Pure Adventure!
      Our spacious campgrounds are divided into three exciting zones: Beach & Water, Survival, and Creative. Go kayaking, bounce on a water trampoline, build rafts, make fires, or climb through the high ropes course – there’s non-stop action every day!

      International Vibes & Real Camp Spirit!
      Our camp is bilingual (English & German) – meet campers from all over the world and improve your English naturally as you go!

      Whether you’re a language lover, outdoor explorer, or creative soul – discover all the profiles and activities that make your camp experience one-of-a-kind.
      We can’t wait to meet you!

      Camp Life – Like a Little Village!

      At our international summer camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between our cozy tent village or the comfy Adventure Lodges – it all depends on your sense of adventure!
      🏕️ Tent Village: Spacious tents for 6–7 campers with wooden floors and a loft area – the ultimate outdoor experience under the stars.
      🏡 Adventure Lodges: Comfortable cabins with 4–8 beds, storage shelves, and seating areas. (Please note: staying in a lodge comes at an extra charge.)
      Accommodations are separated by gender, so girls and boys stay in their own tents or lodges. We also organize by age group to make sure everyone feels right at home:

      • Junior (7–12 years)
      • Senior (12–15 years)
      • Senior Plus (15–17 years)

      Restroom and shower facilities are also separated by gender and always close by.
      Best of all: Our teamers live right next door – they’re available for you 24/7!
      Good to know:
      For tents, bring your own sleeping bag and sleeping mat.
      For lodges, bring a fitted sheet and either a sleeping bag or bedding set (available for rent if needed).
      You can choose your preferred accommodation during the booking process – secure your spot now!

      A Full Day of Adventure, Sports & Creativity!

      "Adventure, Sports & Creativity" is the base program at our Lüneburger Heide Camp – no extra booking needed! If you don’t choose an additional profile like Horseback Riding or Survival, this is your go-to for an action-packed and varied camp experience.

      Every morning, you get to pick a new exciting activity – whatever you’re in the mood for!

      ⛺ Outdoor Action & Adventure: High ropes course, archery, raft building, or survival training – challenge your limits!
      ⚽ Sports & Movement: Soccer, volleyball, basketball, or splashing around in the lake – get moving and have fun!
      🎨 Creativity & Chill: Crafts, painting, reading, or baking – perfect for relaxing moments at camp.

      Learn English – Without Even Trying!
      Our international team brings the real camp spirit – full of energy, adventure, and fun. And the best part? English becomes a natural part of the day – whether you're playing sports, doing creative projects, or chilling by the campfire.

      Friendships That Last!
      Shared adventures create real connections – and many campers already plan their return together for next summer. These are friendships that stick!

      There’s no room for boredom here – every day brings fresh adventures, new sports challenges, and creative highlights just for you!

      Food Everyone Loves!

      Three delicious meals a day – fresh, varied, and super tasty! Whether you’re vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-intolerant – just let us know in advance, and we’ve got you covered.
      Start your day with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh bread rolls, cold cuts, fruit, muesli, juice, and tea.
      At lunch and dinner, enjoy warm meals along with a colorful salad bar.
      Throughout the day, you’ll stay fueled with fresh fruit, afternoon snacks, and plenty of water – perfect for all your camp adventures!
      And the best part? Everything is freshly cooked – no instant soups, no bland cafeteria food. Just real meals that taste great and give you the energy you need!

      Cared for Around the Clock!

      Our experienced and passionate teamers are there for you 24/7 – full of energy, positivity, and always ready to listen. Whether it’s a quick question or a bigger worry, you can count on them anytime.

      The best part?
      Our team comes from all over the world and brings that true international camp spirit – that’s why we speak both English and German!

      This way, you’ll naturally pick up both languages – while playing sports, chatting by the campfire, or just hanging out.
      Our supervision ratio is between 1:7 and 1:10, so you’re always in good hands with our all-around care package!

        Welcome to Regen – Your Adventure in the Bavarian Forest!

        Our Camp Adventure in Regen is located right in the heart of the Bavarian Forest – one of South Germany’s most beautiful natural landscapes. Surrounded by forests, rolling hills, and crystal-clear rivers, it’s the perfect place for adventure, sports, and creative camps in southern Germany.

        Pure action & nature!
        Whether it’s canoeing, kayaking, climbing, or team-building – this is where the fun really begins!
        In cooperation with the Bavarian Sports Association (BLSV), we use top-class sports facilities on-site and nearby to offer even more variety and excitement.

        International community & real camp spirit!
        Our bilingual teamers (English/German) create an inspiring and welcoming atmosphere.

        Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, sports lover, or language explorer – this camp is one-of-a-kind!
        We can’t wait to welcome you!

        Stay in the Heart of Nature!

        At our international summer camp in Bavaria, you get to choose how you want to sleep: in our tent village, cozy tiny houses at the campsite, or in lodges and multi-bed rooms at the BLSV grounds – just the way your adventurous heart likes it!

        🏕️ Tent Village – For true camp vibes!
        Sleep under the stars in 6–7 person tents with wooden floors and a loft-style upper level.

        🏡 Tiny Houses & Cabins – Cozy and practical!
        Each tiny house or cabin features bunk beds, small storage shelves, and a table – space for up to 6 campers.

        🏠 Lodges & Multi-bed Rooms at the BLSV Sportcamp
        Modern 2–6 bed rooms with bunk beds or spacious log cabins with private bathrooms and a lounge area – perfect for groups of up to 12 campers!

        All accommodations are separated by gender and age group:
        Junior (7–12 years) & Senior (12–16 years)
        Our teamers live right next door and are there for you 24/7!
        Restrooms and showers are also separated by gender and always nearby.

        Good to know:
        If you’re staying in a tent, please bring a sleeping bag and sleeping mat.
        For indoor accommodations, bring a fitted sheet and either a sleeping bag or your own bedding – or rent a bedding set on-site if you prefer.
