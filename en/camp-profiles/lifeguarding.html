Safe Rescues: Knowledge and Practice Combined in Camp Adventure’s Lifeguard Course

The perfect course for all kids and teens who want to help others in and around the water! In our lifeguard course, you’ll learn how to safely rescue people in danger—whether they can swim or not. This requires not only physical fitness but also theoretical knowledge and practical skills. Together, you’ll learn to recognize hazardous situations early, make the right decisions, and provide effective help. By the end of the week, you’ll be fully prepared to take the “German Lifeguard Badge – Bronze” exam from the DLRG.

So, head to the Lüneburg Heath—earn this great qualification and become a lifesaver!

    Everything at a Glance

    • Lifeguard Training: 4 days, 2.5 hours each
    • Age Group: 12–17 years
    • Theory and Practice Combined: Gain theoretical knowledge and practical skills for rescue in and around water
    • Preparation for the “German Lifeguard Badge – Bronze” exam by the DLRG, with the option to take the test
    • Full-day diverse program: Community & adventure activities including canoeing, archery, climbing, and
    • swimming pool visits
    • Fun evening program: Campfires, bread roasting, Capture the Flag, and more
    • Cool trips and excursions for two-week stays: Hiking with overnight camping or a city trip to Hamburg
    • Camp Languages English/German: Learn languages playfully in an international atmosphere
    • Full board for 7 days (Sunday to Saturday), 6 nights, with freshly prepared meals
    • 24-hour all-around care: Our counselors are always there for you
    • Digital Detox Approach: Phones and electronic devices allowed only during siesta

    Arrival & Departure

    All Roads Lead to Camp!

    Whether by car, train, plane, or shuttle, your child will arrive safely and comfortably. We take care of everything to ensure a smooth process!

    • Self-arrival: Simply drop off and pick up your child yourself. Detailed directions will be sent by email 6 weeks before camp starts—so nothing stands in the way of a relaxed arrival.
    • Shuttle service: Since our adventure camps are usually in remote areas, we’re happy to provide shuttles from nearby airports and train stations.


    Shuttles run only on Sundays for arrival and Saturdays for departure within set time windows. Outside these times, you can book a private shuttle for an additional fee. Please contact us individually for arrangements.

    Book Your Lifeguard Training & Certification

    Your Lifeguard Course in the Lüneburg Heath!

    Start your adventure now at our Camp Adventure in Northern Germany! Your profile is already selected right here in the booking form—just add your accommodation, arrival, and departure details.

    Secure your spot now and begin your lifeguard adventure!

    And if you want to explore even more: stay an extra week and try another exciting program!

    Welcome to Walsrode – Your Adventure in the Lüneburg Heath!

    Our Camp Adventure (Academy) in Walsrode is beautifully situated in the Lüneburg Heath, right between Bremen, Hamburg, and Hanover. Just a short walk away, you’ll find a charming North German village with farms and half-timbered houses—the perfect place for little discoveries.

    Over 150,000 m² of pure adventure! Our spacious camp area is divided into three exciting zones: Beach/Water, Survival, and Creative. Kayaking, jumping on water trampolines, building rafts, making fires, or climbing in the high ropes course—there’s always action happening here!

    International Community & Camp Spirit! Our camp is bilingual (English & German)—you’ll meet participants from all over the world and improve your English naturally along the way.

    Whether you’re a language enthusiast, outdoor adventurer, or creative mind—discover all the profiles and activities that make your camp experience unique with us! We look forward to seeing you!

    Living Like in a Small Village!

    At our international summer camp in Lower Saxony, you can choose between tent village and adventure lodges—whatever fits your spirit of adventure!
    Tent Village: Six- to seven-person tents with wooden floors and lofts offer genuine outdoor feeling under the stars.
    Adventure Lodges: Cozy cabins with four to eight beds, shelves, and seating areas. (Note: staying in a cabin is an extra charge.)

    Our accommodations are separated by gender, so girls and boys sleep in their own tents or cabins. They are also divided by age groups:

    • Junior (7–12 years)
    • Senior (12–15 years)
    • Senior Plus (15–17 years)


    Bathrooms are also gender-separated and always nearby.

    And the best part: our counselors live right next door and are there for you around the clock!

    Good to know:
    For tents, you need a sleeping bag and sleeping mat; for solid accommodations, a fitted sheet with sleeping bag or bedding, which you can rent if you wish.

    All accommodation options can be selected during the booking process—secure your spot now!

    Become Lifesavers...

    ...and experience an adventure in and around the water! In our lifeguard camp, you’ll confidently earn the DLRG Bronze Badge with ease. Four mornings a week, 2.5 hours each, you’ll train intensively—from hazard recognition to safe rescue techniques. Get ready for exciting practical and theoretical insights that prepare you for any emergency—not just at the North and Baltic Seas!

    Our different programs run in parallel, so you can choose your main focus each week.

    In the afternoons, you can pick from a variety of exciting activities—depending on the weather and availability, there’s always something new to discover! Look forward to highlights in sports, creativity, and survival skills. Evenings continue with a vibrant program full of fun and adventure.

    Food Everyone Will Love!

    Three meals a day—fresh, varied, and truly delicious! Whether vegetarian, gluten-free, or lactose-free options are needed, there’s something for everyone with advance notice.

    Start your day with a hearty breakfast buffet: fresh rolls, cold cuts, fruit, muesli, juices, and tea. Lunch and dinner usually include warm dishes and a colorful salad bar. In between, you can refuel with fresh fruit, an afternoon snack, and plenty of water—perfect for all your adventures!

    And the best part? We always cook fresh! No instant soups, no bland meals—just great food that tastes good and gives you energy!

    Comprehensive Care You Can Count On!

    Our experienced counselors are available around the clock—with fun, good spirits, and always a listening ear. Whether you have small questions or big concerns, you can reach out to them anytime.

    The best part? Our counselors bring true camp spirit from all around the world—that’s why we speak both English and German!

    This way, you naturally immerse yourself in two languages—whether during sports, around the campfire, or just chatting. Our counselor-to-camper ratio ranges from 1:7 to 1:10. With our 24-hour all-around care package, you’re always in the best hands!

    This is exactly how a summer vacation for kids and teens should be! Beautiful grounds with its own lake, beach, and plenty of space all around. And all of this in a relaxed, international atmosphere… Friendly counselors from around the world, great activities, and a full-day program that leaves the kids crawling into their sleeping bags at night full of memories and excited for the next day.
