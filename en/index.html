Go and Grow

Holidays that do more – International language and specialty camps for kids & teens

Outdoor adventures, language travel & unforgettable experiences in Germany, England & Spain – bilingual, media-free & full of camp vibes.

For over 20 years, Go and Grow has stood for international summer camps that truly connect kids and teens. Founded by Jan Vieth and inspired by Canadian camp life, we bring young people from all over the world together – to learn, grow, and explore.

Our language camps, sports camps, and specialty camps combine nature, community, and real learning by living. Here, you don’t just learn a language – you learn life itself: outdoors, together, for real.

Because with us, it’s not just about what you learn – it’s about what you experience.

Holiday camps for every season | Go and Grow

Our outdoor, sports, and language camps run all year round – in Germany, England, and Spain.
When will your next adventure begin?

Spring, Easter & Whitsun – kick off your holidays full of energy!
Our spring camps add some excitement to the short breaks: bilingual, active, and packed with that true camp feeling – from tent camps to language holidays in Germany or Spain.
Perfect to boost your English, Spanish, or German skills – in class or out in nature during epic outdoor adventures.

Summer, summer camps – ready to go?
When the sun’s out, Go and Grow is in its element!
Our summer camps in Germany, Spain, and England offer endless variety: watersports, horseback riding, climbing, football, creative camps, and so much more.
With our bilingual teamers, you’ll improve your English naturally – the best prep for a confident school start!
Or dive straight into language and adventure with our English and Spanish language camps and youth travel programs in Germany, Barcelona, or England.

Autumn, fall camps – days full of action?
Our autumn camp in Walsrode has become a true tradition – authentic Canadian camp life with kids from all over the world.
With over 20 activities, international teamers, and special programs like Husky, horseback riding, or English camps, there’s something for everyone.

Winter, winter break – when the world slows down?
We’re busy creating new ideas, training our teamers, and planning fresh adventures – ready to welcome you back to camp next season.

The perfect camp for every age group

At Go and Grow , kids and teens from all over the world come together – aged 7 to 17. Every age group brings its own unique spirit, and not every camp fits everyone – that’s why we offer a wide range of summer camps and youth travel programs for every age and interest!

Many start with our kids’ camps and holiday programs, experiencing for the first time what it’s like to spend a week away from home. It’s a chance to grow courage, confidence, and real friendships – that’s pure camp life. Our daily structure and activities are tailored to younger campers, helping them feel safe, supported, and right at home.

For older participants, we offer summer camps, specialty programs, and international youth trips that encourage independence, adventure, and new connections – always active, inspiring, and full of opportunities to grow.

Depending on the location and program, age groups mix differently – sometimes younger, sometimes older, but always international. In our outdoor, language, and adventure camps, everyone finds the perfect space to learn, explore, and have fun.

Our specialty camps nurture talents, interests, and self-confidence – with no pressure, just plenty of motivation. Whether you’re a first-time camper or already a seasoned pro, you’ll be seen, supported, and appreciated – just as you are.

Specialty camps & adventure holidays | Go and Grow

Our outdoor, sports, and creative camps combine movement, community, and adventure – all powered by true passion. Whether you want to try something new, develop your talent, or simply experience unforgettable holidays, you’ll find your personal highlight in our specialty camps.

Choose from over 20 different specialty camps across multiple locations – from action to mindfulness, from sports to language. The following examples give you just a taste of our diverse camp world:

Football Camp: Train with real coaches from our partner clubs, boost your skills, team spirit, and game understanding – all in an authentic camp atmosphere.
Multiwater Camp: Sailing, windsurfing, diving, or SUP – it’s all about sun, waves, and endless water fun.
Climbing Camp: Big climbs, high ropes, and our legendary climbing park – with experienced guides, you’ll find out what you’re truly capable of.
Riding Camp: For all horse lovers – from grooming and care to riding technique. Intensive, warm-hearted, and full of horse passion.
Husky Camp: Connection, trust, and responsibility – experience pure energy side by side with our huskies. Action, mindfulness, and teamwork combined.
Leadership Camp: For teens ready to grow beyond themselves – with team challenges, communication training, and a wilderness hike that’ll definitely push you out of your comfort zone.

And if you don’t choose a specialty camp? No worries! Every Go and Grow program includes our signature mix of adventure, sports, and creativity – designed to spark movement, imagination, and team spirit for every age and interest.

Because at Go and Grow , one thing’s always true: no matter what you choose, you’ll discover new things, build friendships, and make memories that last a lifetime.

Find your specialty camp:
→ Football, watersports, horseback riding, huskies, climbing, or leadership – your adventure is waiting!

Language camps & adventure trips | Go and Grow

At Go and Grow , language isn’t just taught – it’s lived. We go beyond vocabulary and grammar to focus on real-life communication, culture, and adventure – true Learning by Living.

Our language programs take place in Germany, England, and Spain – for all ages and levels. Whether you’re just starting with English or preparing for the TOEFL®, we’ll help you grow from where you are.

Language is more than grammar drills. Our native-speaking teamers take you out of the classroom and into real experiences: playing football in English, exploring cities in Spanish, connecting with locals – that’s how your language holiday becomes a true adventure trip.

In Bath and Cornwall, you’ll enjoy multi-day excursions to London. In Barcelona, you’re right in the heart of the city. And our German locations are hand-picked to feel just as adventurous.

Program overview:
• English Classic – practical, everyday English with native coaches
• German Classic – hands-on learning for beginners & advanced learners
• Spanish Classic – experience language, sunshine, and culture
• TOEFL® Course – focused exam preparation with intensive training

Find your language camp:
→ English, Spanish, or German – in Germany, England, or Spain. Language, culture, adventure – all in one camp experience!

School trips & group travel with Go and Grow

Whether it’s a school trip, group travel, or project days – at Go and Grow , students aged 7 to 17 experience unforgettable days full of teamwork, outdoor fun, and international spirit.

Our proven camp concept is perfectly tailored for school groups:

• Age-appropriate programs, clear daily routines, and diverse activities help every class grow closer together.
• Whether it’s outdoor challenges, creative workshops, or teambuilding programs – at Camp Adventure, it’s all about connection and collaboration.
• Teachers can relax: our internationally trained teamers take care of supervision and educational guidance, building confidence and strengthening group bonds.

That’s how every school trip becomes a true adventure journey – combining community, exploration, and learning.

Our locations in Germany, England, and Spain offer the perfect setting: spacious outdoor areas, a wide range of activities, modern facilities, and plenty of room for real camp life.

We also welcome international schools! Together, we create learning spaces where cultures, languages, and perspectives meet.
Openness and global exchange are part of our DNA – here, the world becomes tangible on a small scale: kids and teens from over 70 nations come together, learn from one another, and discover what global community truly means.

Family weekends & parent-child camps at Go and Grow

Real camp life – this time, together!
At our family weekends, you’ll enjoy three days full of adventure, campfires, nature, and connection. Whether it’s canoeing, climbing, swimming, stand-up paddling, or archery – a fun-filled program awaits you from morning till night.

The best part? You don’t have to plan a thing! Accommodation, meals, and activities are all included – true to the Go and Grow motto: arrive, relax, experience.

Our international teamers create the perfect camp vibe, guide you through every activity, and make sure it all feels like a real vacation.
Ideal for families who want to get a taste of camp life, spend quality time together, and share that unique Go and Grow feeling with their kids.

Tip: We also offer father-child camps – perfect for creating lasting memories and sharing unforgettable adventures together.
